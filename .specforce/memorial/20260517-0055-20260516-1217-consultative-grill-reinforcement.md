# Reinforcement of the Consultative Grill

The passive clarification interview was transformed into a proactive, adversarial 'Consultative Grill'. This new standard mandates that all planning phases must include a rigorous technical interrogator persona to challenge design logic, eliminate 'Happy Path' bias, and ensure strict alignment with the Constitution and Codebase before any artifacts are finalized. It also introduced the 'Termination Signal' which requires zero logic contradictions and zero unresolved doubts before proceeding.
